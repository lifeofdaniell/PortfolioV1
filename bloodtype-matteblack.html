PHOTOGRAPHY

BloodType — MatteBlack

YEAR

2019

December 2019; I headed out to the beach with some friends, my camera, a white fabric and an oval shaped mirror. I hadn't figured out exactly what I wanted to do with those equipment but I knew once I got to the beach and relaxed a little with J.Cole in the background, it would hit me.

3 hours and a broken mirror later, we created magic!

This series of images showcases how boldly the dark skin stands out from any white material, surface or fabric.

Muse: Amos Adeyemi & Adekemi Adeniji
Assisted by: Leon Izegbu, Nkechi Okochi & Temileyi Iwelu

RAW images from Canon EOS200D with the EF-S18-55mm f/3.5-5.6 III and EF50mm f/1.8STM lens.

You can follow my Instagram page or my Behance page where I will be sharing more images from my photography journey.
